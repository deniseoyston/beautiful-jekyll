## The York Herald, Friday, March 9 1894; page 4

##### Deaths
##### Glaves Prince of West Ayton

On the 7th inst., after 18 months' illness of dropsy, Mr. Glaves Prince, farmer of High Yedmandale, West Ayton, aged 67 years. To be interred at Hutton Buscel to-day (Friday) at 3.30 p.m. Friends please accept this, the only invitation.
